COMPANY POLICY - LEAVE MANAGEMENT
Employees can apply for leave via the HR portal.
Annual leave quota is 20 days per year.
Leave requests must be submitted at least 3 days in advance.
Emergency leave can be applied same day with manager approval.
Handover document must be submitted before any leave exceeding 2 days.

COMPANY POLICY - MEETINGS
All client meetings must have an agenda shared 24 hours before.
Meeting notes must be recorded and shared within 2 hours after the meeting.
Virtual meetings require a stable internet connection and professional background.
Client meetings must include at least one senior team member.

COMPANY POLICY - HR
New employees complete a 3-month probation period.
Performance reviews are conducted every 6 months.
Training budget is $500 per employee per year.
Remote work is allowed up to 2 days per week.

COMPANY POLICY - PROJECTS
All projects must have a designated project lead.
Weekly status updates must be shared with the team every Monday.
Project deadlines must be approved by the project manager.
Code reviews are mandatory before any deployment.
